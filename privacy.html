Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

What we collect

Nothing. Agentiz does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no cookies set by us, and no analytics tracking.

Local storage

Some preferences (like your chosen tab cloak) are saved locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-party content

When you use the workspace to browse websites, those websites may set their own cookies and collect data according to their own privacy policies. Agentiz has no control over third-party content.

No logs

We do not log IP addresses, browsing history, or usage data of any kind.
